Rateable Value Appeals in Glasgow

Think your business rates are too high? We challenge incorrect rateable value assessments to reduce your rates bill and secure refunds where applicable.

Reduce Your Business Rates

Business rates are based on your property's rateable value, assessed by the Scottish Assessors. Many businesses pay more than they should due to incorrect valuations. We help you challenge these assessments through the proper appeals process.

When to Appeal

  • Property changes – Physical alterations affecting value
  • Local changes – Roadworks, construction, or declining area
  • Comparable evidence – Similar properties rated lower
  • New revaluation – After a revaluation year
  • Errors – Incorrect measurements or property details

Our Appeals Service

  • Free initial assessment – We review your current valuation
  • Evidence gathering – Comparable valuations and property analysis
  • Appeal submission – Formal proposal to the Assessor
  • Negotiation – Discussion with the Assessor's office
  • Tribunal representation – If formal hearing required
  • Refund claims – Recover overpaid rates if successful

Available Reliefs

You may also qualify for rates relief:

  • Small Business Bonus Scheme – Up to 100% relief
  • Empty property relief – For vacant premises
  • Charitable relief – 80% mandatory for charities
  • Fresh Start relief – For reoccupied empty properties
