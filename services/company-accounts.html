Company Accounts Preparation in Glasgow

Professional preparation of annual accounts and financial statements. We ensure compliance with Companies House requirements and accounting standards.

Complete Accounts Service

Every UK limited company must prepare and file annual accounts with Companies House. These accounts provide a snapshot of your company's financial position and must comply with UK accounting standards. We prepare professional, accurate accounts that meet all statutory requirements.

What We Provide

  • Statutory annual accounts – Full accounts compliant with UK GAAP/FRS 102
  • Micro-entity accounts – Simplified accounts for qualifying small companies
  • Abridged accounts – Reduced disclosure accounts for eligible companies
  • Companies House filing – Electronic submission on your behalf
  • Director's report – Required narrative for larger companies
  • Management accounts – Monthly/quarterly financial reports for decision-making

Filing Deadlines

Missing deadlines results in automatic penalties:

  • Private companies – 9 months after your accounting period ends
  • Public companies – 6 months after your accounting period ends

Late Filing Penalties

Companies House charges escalating penalties for late accounts:

  • Up to 1 month late: £150
  • 1-3 months late: £375
  • 3-6 months late: £750
  • Over 6 months late: £1,500

Penalties double if accounts are filed late two years in a row.

Our Process

  • 1. Gather information – We collect your bookkeeping records and bank statements
  • 2. Review and reconcile – We check everything balances correctly
  • 3. Prepare accounts – We produce compliant financial statements
  • 4. Director approval – You review and approve the accounts
  • 5. File with Companies House – We submit electronically
