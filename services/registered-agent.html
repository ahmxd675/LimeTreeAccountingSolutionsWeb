Companies House Registered Agent

Professional registered agent services in Glasgow. We provide a registered office address, handle official correspondence, and ensure your company stays compliant.

Your Trusted Registered Agent

Every UK limited company must have a registered office address where official correspondence is sent. As a Companies House registered agent, we provide a professional Glasgow address and handle all official mail on your behalf, keeping your home address private and ensuring nothing gets missed.

Registered Office Service

  • Glasgow business address – Professional registered office
  • Mail handling – Receive and forward official post
  • Immediate notification – Alert you of important documents
  • Privacy protection – Keep your home address off public records
  • Companies House filings – Address change and updates

Compliance Services

  • Confirmation Statement – Annual filing with Companies House
  • PSC Register – Persons with Significant Control updates
  • Director changes – Appointments and resignations
  • Share transactions – Allotments and transfers
  • Statutory registers – Maintain company records
  • Deadline reminders – Never miss a filing date

Benefits of Using a Registered Agent
