How much does an accountant cost for a sole trader?

Our sole trader packages start from very competitive rates. We offer fixed-fee pricing for self-assessment returns so you know exactly what you'll pay. Contact us for a free quote based on your situation.

What expenses can I claim as self-employed?

You can claim any expense that's "wholly and exclusively" for your business. This includes tools, materials, vehicle costs, phone, insurance, marketing, training, and a proportion of home costs if you work from home.

When is the self-assessment deadline?
