Professional Lease Documentation

A well-drafted commercial lease protects both landlords and tenants. We prepare comprehensive lease documentation that clearly sets out the terms, responsibilities, and rights of all parties, helping to prevent disputes and misunderstandings.

For Landlords

  • New lease preparation – Comprehensive lease documentation
  • Rent review clauses – Clear terms for rent adjustments
  • Break clauses – Fair terms for early termination
  • Repair obligations – Clear allocation of responsibilities
  • Service charge provisions – Transparent cost recovery

For Tenants

  • Lease review – Understanding your obligations before signing
  • Negotiation support – Advice on terms to negotiate
  • Assignment/subletting – Understanding your rights
  • Dilapidations advice – End of lease obligations

Key Lease Terms We Cover

  • Lease term and renewals
  • Rent and payment terms
  • Permitted use of premises
  • Insurance requirements
  • Alterations and improvements
  • Termination provisions
