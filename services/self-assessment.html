Who Needs Self-Assessment?

You'll need to file a Self-Assessment tax return if you:

  • Are self-employed as a sole trader earning over £1,000
  • Are a partner in a business partnership
  • Earn rental income from property
  • Have investment income (dividends, savings interest over £10,000)
  • Earn over £100,000 per year
  • Have capital gains to report
  • Need to claim tax reliefs (e.g., Gift Aid over £100)
  • Are a company director

Key Deadlines

Don't miss these important Self-Assessment deadlines:

  • 5 October – Register for Self-Assessment if you're new
  • 31 October – Paper return deadline (if applicable)
  • 31 January – Online return deadline and tax payment due
  • 31 July – Second payment on account (if applicable)
