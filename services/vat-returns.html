Do You Need to Register for VAT?

You must register for VAT if:

  • Your VAT taxable turnover exceeds £90,000 in any 12-month period
  • You expect to exceed the threshold in the next 30 days
  • You receive goods from the EU worth more than £90,000

Voluntary registration may benefit some businesses – we can advise.
