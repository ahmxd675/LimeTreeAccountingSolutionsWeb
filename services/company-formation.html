Company Formation in Glasgow

Start your limited company with expert guidance. We handle Companies House registration, share structure, and set you up for success from day one.

Start Your Business the Right Way

Setting up a limited company offers significant benefits including limited liability protection, tax efficiency, and enhanced credibility. As a Companies House registered agent, we make the formation process quick, easy, and hassle-free.

What's Included

  • Company name check – We verify your chosen name is available
  • Companies House registration – Full incorporation with all documents
  • Certificate of Incorporation – Your official company registration document
  • Memorandum & Articles – Standard or tailored governing documents
  • Share structure setup – Directors, shareholders, and share allocation
  • HMRC registration – Corporation Tax and PAYE if needed

Benefits of a Limited Company

  • Limited liability – Your personal assets are protected
  • Tax efficiency – Pay yourself through salary and dividends
  • Professional image – Increased credibility with clients
  • Business continuity – Company exists independently of you

Our Process

  • 1. Initial consultation – Discuss your business plans and structure
  • 2. Name check – Verify availability and suitability
  • 3. Registration – Submit to Companies House (usually same day)
  • 4. Setup complete – Receive your documents and start trading
