Company Dissolution in Glasgow

Need to close your limited company? We handle the entire dissolution process, ensuring compliance with Companies House and HMRC requirements.

Close Your Company Properly

When a company is no longer needed, it must be formally closed to avoid ongoing filing requirements, potential fines, and director liabilities. We guide you through the process, ensuring everything is done correctly and efficiently.

Dissolution Options

  • Voluntary strike off (DS01) – For solvent companies with no debts or assets
  • Members' Voluntary Liquidation – For companies with assets to distribute
  • Creditors' Voluntary Liquidation – For insolvent companies with debts

Requirements for Strike Off

Your company can apply for voluntary strike off if it:

  • Has not traded or sold stock in the last 3 months
  • Has not changed its name in the last 3 months
  • Is not subject to any legal proceedings
  • Has no outstanding debts, assets, or liabilities

Our Dissolution Service Includes

  • Eligibility assessment – We check if strike off is right for you
  • Final accounts preparation – Complete your statutory obligations
  • Final Corporation Tax return – Notify HMRC of cessation
  • DS01 application – Strike off application to Companies House
  • Creditor notifications – Inform relevant parties as required
  • Confirmation of dissolution – Company removed from register

Important Considerations

Before dissolving your company, consider:

  • Outstanding tax liabilities must be settled first
  • Assets over £25,000 may require formal liquidation
  • Overdrawn director's loan accounts need attention
  • Capital gains may apply on distributions
