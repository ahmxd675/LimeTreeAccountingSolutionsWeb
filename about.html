About LimeTree Accounting Solutions

Professional, personal accounting services for individuals and businesses across Glasgow.

Your Trusted Glasgow Accountants

LimeTree Accounting Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional, affordable accounting services with a personal touch. Based in the heart of Glasgow, we understand the unique challenges faced by local businesses and individuals navigating the UK tax system.

Unlike large accountancy firms where you might never speak to the same person twice, at LimeTree you get direct access to qualified accountants who truly understand your situation. We take the time to listen, explain things in plain English, and provide solutions tailored to your specific needs.

As an AAT accredited practice and registered Companies House agent, we combine professional qualifications with practical experience to deliver accounting services you can rely on.
